About Us

Negash Lodge offers a peaceful natural retreat, quality accommodation, and relaxing services for both local and international guests.

Nestled in the beautiful landscapes of Woliso, our lodge provides the perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. Whether you're looking for a romantic getaway, a family vacation, or a business retreat, we have the perfect accommodations and amenities to make your stay memorable.

Our dedicated staff is committed to providing exceptional service and ensuring that your experience with us is nothing short of extraordinary.
